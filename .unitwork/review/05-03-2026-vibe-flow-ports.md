### Cleanliness Issues

#### 1. TEMPLATE_DRIFT - spec.md missing sections from inline template

**Location:** `plugins/unitwork/skills/unitwork/templates/spec.md`

**Issue:** The standalone spec template is missing two sections that exist in the inline template within `uw-plan.md`:
- "Linear Ticket" section (with Ticket ID and URL fields)
- "Assumptions (To Verify During Implementation)" section

The inline template in `uw-plan.md` (lines 498-524) includes both sections, but the standalone `templates/spec.md` does not. This means specs generated manually from the template vs generated by `/uw:plan` will have different structures.

**Why:** TEMPLATE_DRIFT — two sources of truth for the same template diverge.

**Fix:**
Add both missing sections to `templates/spec.md`:
- "Linear Ticket" section after the title
- "Assumptions (To Verify During Implementation)" section after "Out of Scope"

#### 2. MISSING_DOCUMENTATION - SKILL.md Commands section missing /uw:pr-review

**Location:** `plugins/unitwork/skills/unitwork/SKILL.md` (Commands section, lines ~206-217)

**Why:** MISSING_DOCUMENTATION — new feature not reflected in reference documentation.
